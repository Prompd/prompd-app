## Recording Tips Reminder

1. **Pause 2 seconds** at video start and end (editing buffer)
2. **Move cursor slowly** - viewers need to track it
3. **Hold on callout moments** - give text time to register
4. **Don't rush typing** - ~60 WPM max, viewers are reading
5. **Multiple takes are fine** - you'll edit anyway
6. **Check recording after each scene** - easier to redo immediately
